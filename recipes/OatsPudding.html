Fruity Oats Pudding

Description

Looking for a delicious and no-bake pudding to make your breakfast interesting, then try this simple yet sweet oats recipe. To make this easy recipe, you just need some oats, hung curd, vanilla essence, berries and brown sugar. You can accentuate the taste of this pudding by adding your favourite ingredients and indulge in its delightful taste with your loved ones.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup oats
  • 2 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 handfuls strawberry
  • 1 handfuls blueberry
  • 1 1/2 cup hung curd
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla essence
  • 1 handfuls raspberry

Steps

  1. Soak oats

    2. Take a large bowl and add milk and soak the oats. Keep it overnight in a refrigerator.

  2. Make a curd mixture

    3. Take a large bowl, add hung curd along with sugar using a whisker, beat the curd into a smooth mixture. Then add in vanilla essence and mix it well.

  3. Garnish and enjoy

    4. Layer the pudding, take out the soaked oats and pour the curd mixture, toss it well. Garnish with berries and enjoy.
